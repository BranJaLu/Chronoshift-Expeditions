Expeditions

General Information

Chronoshift Expeditions gives its visiters the chance to travel into the past. This allows every fan of prehistorical organism to see them in person when they were still around. Customers are also given permission to pet these organisms though they have to make gloves while doing so.

It is also important to make sure not to cause a rift to the timeline of the history of Earth. Doing so can be a problem back in the present. That's why visiters need to pay close attention to all the rules and policies to protect the past.

Expeditions

Cretaceous

The Creatacceous Period lasted from 145 to 66 million years ago. It was both the thrid and final period of the Mesozoic Era as well as being the longest. This period along with the Mesozoic period ended with the Creataceous-Palogene extinction event which killed off most of the dinosaurs with the exception of some avian dinosaurs which would become brids. The Creatacceous period would be followed by the Cenozoic Era which is still going on today.

Origin of life

Also known in biology as abiogenesis, is the natural process that life arisen from non-living matter such as organic compunds. The earliest organisms are unknown but they are believed to appear on Earth at least 3.37 Billion years ago althought it could also be 4.28 billion years or even 4.42 billion years. These early organisms would product offsprings and as time goes back, their offsprings would develop unique traits that are essential to their environment and survival. Over time, each group of similar would diverge from each other to established their own group.
