About Chronoshift Expeditions

About Chronoshift Expeditions

Chronoshift Expeditions takes its viewers into the past. Unlike Jurrassic Park, the experience is filled with actual organisms in the past. It is incredible to see these events played out in person. Only the ones who are good listeners and know about history can experienced these safaris/

The company was find in 2025 and has gave safari tours to over a million people worldwide in the past. The numbers are expected to get higher as more and more want to see what the past looked like.

Managing safaris is very expensive because it's not only for the equipment used but also to limit the number of visiters. This way the numbers are controlled easily.

Time Travel Technology

Our Time Technology came from an unlikely source. Back during quarantine, Professer Stevenson got a bit bored. Wanting to kill some time, he came up with pranking the internet with an actual time machine for April's Fool Day. There was no way that creating an actual time machine can happened but boy was Professer Stevenson was wrong. While mixing different substances to make his time machine more believeable, something strange happened. The machine started to glow up before sucking in the poor Professer. He was then expelled from the hole and discover he was teleported 2 seconds ago but luckily his past self got sucked into it. Interested he got the machine to work, he called in His co-workers to see the machine for themselves. After experimenting with the machine for a couple of years, he decided to open a safari from a request from his nephew. This was the story how our time technology and Chronoshift Expedition came to be.

The technology works but first glowing up. It requries enough energy for it to travel into any period in time. Without enought power, it could just be sent back seconds ago before it was sent. Good thing Professer Stevenson told us that time stands in to prevent any paradox. Its power source runs with a mix of solar, electrial, biodiesel, and nuclear energy thus it creates a powerful and renewable energy source than any single energy source from around the world.

There have been many accidents with the time travel technology but luckily there have only been injuries and no deaths so far. These accidents are unpredictable and it's better to prepare for any accidents. Overall, Chronoshift research divison is doing their best to fix these problems for the next-gen time machines. Our recent one, The Primer is a perfect creation but there are flaws to it. To know more, read "The Primer" article.

The Primer

A 5th-Gen Time Machine, it has the most latest time travel technology that our research divison has developed to fix the previous mistakes. It has the ability to open up a portal and travel through space and time and locate the exact time period that it is headed to. Running on rails help stablized its balance and can hover if needed.

This has been so far the most expensive product that Chronoshift staffs have faced. It has required a lot of skilled physicist, engineers, and technology specialist from all around the world to build it using raw materials. This brings the price at about $200 Billions. The machine is checked for any maintenance after its safari tour.

Despite its cost, this machine has been proven to be a sucess and few accidents have been reported. However Chronoshift is looking to develop a similar time machine to the Primer but has a low cost while retaining the high quality of the previous machine.
