Expedition Policies

Rules

  1. Always stay on the path!
  2. Never leave the path. You will get lost in the past.
  3. Only interact with permission from the Safari Guides. Make sure to have your hands sanitized and gloves are on.
  4. Leave no trace nor take anything from the past. It could cause a rift in time.
  5. Don't get left behind. You don't want your remains to be find and preserved in a museum.

Policies

Chronoshift Expeditions has the strictest rules in order to presevre the present timeline. One small mistake like stepping on a mouse-like animal could cause a rift in time. Even killing the first organism could erase all organisms including humans from the present day.

Weapons

Since Chronoshift Expeditions does not allowing hunting in the past due to concerns that it could cause a crisis, weapons should only be for defenses only. Weapons given to safari guides and visiters are meant to stun or frighten off any dangers.

Suit

Considering that germs can get into anything and if they get into the past, these could evolved into something worse. Time Travelers are to always wear a protective suit and mask so that way germs are not introduce into the past. This also applies to protect the wearer from dangerous elements in the past.

Taking Pictures

While it's not allowed to take anything from the past, taking a picture is okay. First informed the safari guide that you are taking a picture. If they grant you permission, make sure not to go off the path as stated. The same applies for recording videos.

Time Period

Traveling to any time period of human history is forbidden because it could change the course of human history from bad to even more worse. The period where humans are evolving however is okay. Make sure that you keep your distance away from them.
